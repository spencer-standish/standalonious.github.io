About Us

Help Us Save Canadian Animals!

CAN Save is a non-profit organization based out of Canada that looks to protect, educate people on and promote the many wonderful animals that call Canada home. Founded in 2022 by 5 Humber College students, this website is the first step to reaching out to the broader public on a cause we care so much about.

Canada is the 2nd largest country in the world by land mass, making it home to some of the largest and highest variety of species. Sadly, many of these species are endangered due to over-hunting, global warming and loss of habitat, our goal is to prevent this and reverse the negative trend Canada's fauna is on. By donating to our organization, we can guarantee that your hard earned money will be used responsibly and only go towards our ultimate goal of saving Canadian animals.
